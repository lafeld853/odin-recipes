Ordering food

Description

Ordering food may be your way to go if you don't have the time or the ingredients to cook. You can order your food via your Smartphone and it will arrive within the next hour. You just have to go with the following steps to order your food on Lieferando

Steps

  1. Enter your location on Lieferando to see all available restaurants near you.
  2. Select a restaurant and click on the "i" to the right of the name to open the info.
  3. Briefly check whether your desired delivery time falls within the restaurant's delivery times. Then close the info box again.
  4. Select the desired dishes and then press "Shopping cart". Check your order and press "Pay" (not yet charged).
  5. In the "Delivery time" section, you will be told when you can expect your meal to be delivered. Select the desired time from the drop-down menu to receive the delivery at the desired time.
  6. Complete the order by clicking on "Buy now".
